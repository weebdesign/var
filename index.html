Cultivation and Uses:
Jui plants thrive in warm climates and well-drained soil, requiring ample sunlight to flourish. Gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts cherish Jui for its ornamental value, adorning gardens, trellises, and balconies with its fragrant blooms. Besides its decorative appeal, Jui flowers are also utilized in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits, brewed into teas, or used as essential oils for their therapeutic properties.
